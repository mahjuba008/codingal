Dhaka's roads are often busy and congested, reflecting the city's rapid urban growth and high population density. Major roads like Mirpur Road, Airport Road, and Gulshan Avenue are vital for daily commuting. Despite recent improvements in infrastructure, traffic jams and poor road conditions remain common challenges. Efforts like flyovers and metro rail aim to ease transportation woes.


AHSAN MANJIL

Ahsan Manzil, known as the Pink Palace, is a historic building on the banks of the Buriganga River in Dhaka. Built in the 19th century, it was the residence of the Nawabs of Dhaka. Now a museum, it showcases the rich history and heritage of the city. Its striking pink color and grand architecture make it a popular tourist attraction.
